Smart Minds is a digital learning platform designed for Grade 12 (matric) learners and those upgrading their results. It offers subject-specific learning for Physical Sciences, Mathematics, Life Sciences, Geography, Accounting, Economics, Business Studies, and Mathematical Literacy. The platform is structured exactly according to the matric curriculum, with every subject divided into Paper 1 and Paper 2, and each paper broken down into its correct chapters and topics. Students subscribe per subject at R250 per month, gaining access to live online lessons, chapter-based video lessons, theory quizzes, calculation practice questions, and a complete past-question-paper library. Smart Minds will also include recorded lessons, downloadable study notes, worksheets, and a personalized student dashboard that tracks progress. Teachers and administrators can upload lessons, schedule live sessions, create quizzes, upload past papers, and manage learners. The system is built to be expandable, mobile-friendly, and suitable for long-term growth.
